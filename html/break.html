换行
类 属性 描述
text-wrap-wrap text-wrap: wrap; 允许换行，必要时在单词内换行
text-wrap-nowrap text-wrap: nowrap; 不换行，必要时在单词内换行
text-wrap-balance text-wrap: balance; 在必要时调整行间距，使最后一行更饱满
text-wrap-pretty text-wrap: pretty; 在必要时调整行间距，使段落更易读
break-normal overflow-wrap: normal;word-break: normal; 正常换行
break-word overflow-wrap: break-word; 允许在单词内换行
break-all word-break: break-all; 在任意字符之间换行
text-wrap 的效果 (控制整体排版)

text-wrap 值下的排版差异，尤其是 balance 如何让最后一行更饱满。

wrap (默认)

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

balance (平衡)

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

nowrap (不换行)

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

overflow-wrap & word-break 的效果 (处理长单词)

注意 break-word 和 break-all 的区别。

normal (不处理)

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

单词会溢出容器

overflow-wrap: break-word

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

仅在必要时断词

word-break: break-all

Even the most beautiful flowers wither after blooming.

可任意位置断词
